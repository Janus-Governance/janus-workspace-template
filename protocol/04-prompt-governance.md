# Protocol 04 — Prompt Governance

**Status:** stub  
**Ref:** https://framework.janusgovernance.org/protocol/prompt-governance

---

## Principle

Prompts sent to AI systems are governed artifacts.  
They carry an ID, intent declaration, and expected output specification.

## Required fields

- `PROMPT_ID` — unique identifier  
- declared intent  
- expected output type

## Stub

Adopt naming convention: `PROMPT_ID: <NAMESPACE>-<TOPIC>-<VERSION>`
