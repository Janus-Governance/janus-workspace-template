**Status:** stub  
**Ref:** https://framework.janusgovernance.org/protocol/evidence-first

## Principle

No action is taken before evidence is collected and presented.  
Observation precedes intervention.

## Required events

- `trace` — records that something happened  
- `expect` — declares what should have happened  
- `evaluate` — compares E+ / E−
